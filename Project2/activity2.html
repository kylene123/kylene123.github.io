About Baguio City

Baguio City is a popular tourist destination known for its cool climate, picturesque views, and historical landmarks. Whether you're visiting Burnham Park, Session Road, or Camp John Hay, Baguio offers a blend of nature and culture that draws tourists from all over the country.
